ABOUT US

The term UX Angel is inspired by Trail Angel, a name given to those who dedicate time and energy every year to support those hiking 2663 mi (4286 km) from Mexico to Canada on the Pacific Crest Trail. Like startups and nonprofits, only a small percentage of hikers succeed in their journey. We dedicate our own resources and expertise to projects we recognize as pioneers for a better future.
