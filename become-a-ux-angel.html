Why become a UX Angel?

Three reasons to join our elite team:

  1. Network with other industry professionals
  2. Challenge yourself with new projects and contexts
  3. There are no commitments, just opportunities

How does it work?

Startups approach UXAngels with requests for help (and sometimes we approach them). One or more angels will accept the project and setup a time to meet with the startup to discuss, whiteboard, brainstorm their problem. The startup provides dinner and drinks. It's that simple.
