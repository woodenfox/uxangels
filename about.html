The UXA Team

Mike Messenger (Seattle)

Product manager, front end engineer, and UX designer, Mike managed the launch of Amazon Follow—allowing customers to receive personal release announcements from authors on Amazon. He also launched an Amazon internal version of Qualtrics, a user research and survey tool. As VP of Product at Talenthouse, a social network for artists, he grew the user base from 1K users to >1.2MM and played a principal role in closing our $13.1M Series B.

Dirk Stockton (San Francisco)

Art Director, designer, software developer, and founder of Wit Design, Dirk's goal is to explore and advance the web as it's own medium. Using design to bridge the gap between users and utility. Dirk believes that the world deserves elegant and beautiful design.

What is our story?

UX Angels (UXA) was founded in 2012, in Palo Alto, CA. The group has grown to include angels in Seattle, San Francisco, and Oakland.

The term UX Angel is inspired by Trail Angel, a name given to those who dedicate time and energy every year to support those hiking 2663 mi (4286 km) from Mexico to Canada on the Pacific Crest Trail. Like startups and nonprofits, only a small percentage of hikers succeed in their journey. We dedicate our own resources and expertise to projects we recognize as pioneers for a better future.
